Welcome to a rugby land, ready to rumble?

The ‘Stade Rochelais’ La Rochelle Rugby Club has a rich history spanning more than 100 years of wins, defeats, emotions and unifying moments.

That history mustn't be forgotten, it is our duty to remember and to celebrate this great team!They fight with all their heart if we support them as much as we can! Come and sing!

On this website you'll be able to learn La Rochelle's song, get your supporter's outfit and follow the official club's website

La Rochelle is also a very nice place as a town. Alot of animation on the port or near the market. Become a Rochelais, follow us, join us and we'll make you one of us. send email to : stade_rochelaisTOP@gmail.com in order to become part of the fan club.

La Rochelle's Palmares:

  • winner of the ascension (2010, 2014)
  • League Cup winner (2002, 2003)
  • Finalist "challenge de l'Esperance" (1962,1971)
  • semifinalist European Challenge (2017)
  • quarter finalist European Cup 2018
